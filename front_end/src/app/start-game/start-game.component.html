Welcome to Play Smear

Play the card game Smear online against the computer or friends. Click here for more information and the rules of the game.

How to play with friends or family

One person should go to the "Play against friends" tab and start a game. Then that person should communicate the Game ID to all the other players (via text message, the slack workspace, etc). Finally, all the other players go to the "Join an existing game" tab and enter the Game ID.

Find other players

To find other people to play against, consider joining our Slack workspace! Slack is a free group communication tool that in this case can be used to find other players and coordinate games. Use this invite link or submit feedback (using the "Contact Us" link below) with your email address to request an invite.
